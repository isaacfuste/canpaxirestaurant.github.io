Welcome to Can Paxi

At Can Paxi Restaurant, we are a family passionate about home cooking. We prepare each dish fresh to order with high-quality ingredients, so you can enjoy traditional flavors in a warm and welcoming atmosphere. We specialize in daily menus featuring traditional dishes at competitive prices. We also offer menus for groups and celebrations. Our strength lies in our homemade and healthy cuisine, where we lovingly prepare our dishes, stews, sauces, and desserts. We strive to offer attentive and prompt service, perfect for working professionals and families looking for a delicious and hassle-free meal. Come to Can Paxi Restaurant and enjoy a good home-cooked meal, just like at home!
